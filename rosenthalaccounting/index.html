About Michal

Your Trusted Partner
in Financial Success

Michal Afik-Shifman is a seasoned accounting and tax professional with over 20 years of hands-on experience helping individuals, families, and businesses navigate the complexities of the U.S. tax code.

Based in Rockville, MD, Michal was recognized as the 2023 Best Rated Tax Preparer in Rockville. As an IRS Annual Filing Season Program participant and certified PTIN holder, she brings rigorous professional standards to every engagement — from simple individual returns to complex business filings, expatriate situations, and cryptocurrency portfolios.

She founded Rosenthal Accounting on the belief that great accounting is built on relationships, not just numbers — and that every client deserves expert, personalized attention.
